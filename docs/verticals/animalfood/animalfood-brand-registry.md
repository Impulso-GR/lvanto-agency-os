## 0. How to read this registry

- **Confidence** = how sure we are of the brand's role/data: High / Medium / Low / Needs data.
- **Claims** obey [[animalfood-extraordinary-content-standard]] §18 — only product-fact-backed claims; uncertain → **Needs confirmation**; never invent.
- **Price note:** all price perception is from a **public MercadoLibre reseller audit (2026-06-07)** — **not** official AnimalFood prices, wholesale, or margins. Price tiers below are *positioning lanes*, never numbers to publish.
- **Portfolio rule:** brands must read as **distinct**. Canfeed defends premium through **system completeness + evidence**, never by comparing price with its own value siblings.

**Account map** (per [[animalfood-column-map]] §13): `@animalfoodargentina` (mother/institucional) · `@canfeed.ar` (Canfeed + Catfeed) · `@enercan.ar` (Enercan + Enercat) · `@ironpet.ar` (IronPet dog + cat). Puro / SuperPet / Ulyses have **no confirmed dedicated IG account**.

- **No fixed B2B prices/margins** anywhere — they vary by customer + quantity (C5).

- Uncertainty stays labeled; **Needs data** is a valid, honest status.
