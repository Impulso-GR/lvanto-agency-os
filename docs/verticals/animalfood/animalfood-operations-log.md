## 1. Purpose

This log keeps a practical, honest record of how AnimalFood is actually operated day to day — not a bureaucratic diary. Its job is to help the system become **more useful**: to learn the real working style of Gonzalo and Aranza, recognize recurring tasks, capture commercial and design decisions, surface bottlenecks, and turn one-off work into reusable learnings.

The **Google Sheet** tracks **active work** (what is in motion right now). This file explains **how we operate** (the style, decisions, and lessons behind that work). They are complements, not duplicates.

## 2. What to log

Log any of the following when it happens:

- promotions received from AnimalFood;
- templates prepared for sellers;
- WhatsApp commercial communications;
- content created for each brand (Canfeed, Catfeed, Animalfood institucional);
- tasks assigned to Gonzalo;
- tasks assigned to Aranza;
- design decisions;
- copy decisions;
- client feedback;
- seller feedback;
- metrics or results;
- bottlenecks;
- reusable learnings.

Keep it to what actually matters for selling more, strengthening the brands, and making execution faster and more consistent. If it does not help one of those, it probably does not need a row.

## 3. How to log an action

1. Add **one row** to the Operations Log Table (Section 9).
2. Fill **Date, Source, Brand, Action** at minimum.
3. Capture the **Raw input** (e.g. the WhatsApp message as received) and the **Output created** (the cleaned/usable result).
4. Set **Owner** (Gonzalo / Aranza / AnimalFood / Seller) and **Status**.
5. Record any **Design decision** and **Commercial decision** made.
6. Add **Evidence / Confidence** — what backs this, and High / Medium / Low.
7. Leave **Result, Learning, Next action** to be filled as the action progresses.
8. Keep entries concise — one line of substance per cell, not paragraphs.
9. Do **not** copy active-work rows from the Google Sheet here; link to them by Date+Brand if needed.

## 4. Decision quality rule

The system must **not** suggest changes just to sound smart. Every suggestion must clearly improve at least one of:

- clarity;
- sales conversion;
- seller understanding;
- brand consistency;
- production speed;
- content quality;
- B2B trust;
- B2C demand;
- measurement.

If a suggestion does not clearly improve one of these, do not make it.

## 5. Design improvement rule

When reviewing templates or content, the system may suggest improvements such as:

- increase text size;
- improve hierarchy;
- simplify information;
- reduce visual noise;
- make the CTA clearer;
- make the benefit more concrete;
- improve contrast;
- respect brand identity;
- adapt the format for WhatsApp, story, reel, carousel, or seller use.

**Every design suggestion must include:**
- **Reason** — why the change is proposed;
- **Expected benefit** — what improves;
- **Risk if ignored** — what is lost or weakened by not doing it;
- **Confidence level** — High / Medium / Low.

## 6. Commercial improvement rule

For promotions, the system must help convert raw WhatsApp information into a clean, sellable package:

- **Clear seller message** — what the seller says, in plain words;
- **Simple offer structure** — what is being offered;
- **Benefit for the buyer** — why it matters to them;
- **Conditions** — limits, eligibility, dates, minimums;
- **Urgency if real** — only when genuine, never invented;
- **Recommended format** — WhatsApp text, story, carousel, seller card, etc.;
- **Next action** — who does what next.

The goal is to make the offer easier to sell, not to add steps.

## 7. Evidence / confidence rule

No recommendation should be presented as certain unless it is backed by **real data, platform insight, seller feedback, client feedback, or a prior result**.

If the data is missing:
- label it explicitly as an **assumption**;
- request the **specific missing input**;
- do not present the assumption as fact.

Use **High / Medium / Low** confidence on recommendations, and say plainly when the system is **not sure**.

## 8. Current working style

- **Gonzalo** directs strategy, design quality, visual hierarchy, and final approval.
- **Aranza** adapts templates, prepares variants, uploads/schedules, and helps maintain execution.
- **AnimalFood** sends raw commercial information, often by WhatsApp.
- The system must help **organize and improve** that information **without complicating execution**.
- The goal: **sell more**, **strengthen the AnimalFood brands**, and make Gonzalo and Aranza **faster and more consistent**.
